Dot Product — Measuring Similarity

The dot product tells us how similar two vectors are. It is the core operation in attention: Q·K measures how much a query "attends" to a key.

a · b = |a| × |b| × cos(θ) = a1b1 + a2b2

Softmax — From Scores to Probabilities

Softmax converts raw scores (logits) into a probability distribution that sums to 1. In attention, it turns Q·KT similarity scores into attention weights.

softmax(zi) = ezi/T / ∑j ezj/T

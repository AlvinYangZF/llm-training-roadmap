How Embeddings Are Made

Three eras of embedding models, each solving limitations of the previous one. Click an era to explore.

2013

Word2Vec

Skip-gram & CBOW. One fixed vector per word. 100-300 dimensions.
2018

BERT

Contextual embeddings. Same word gets different vectors based on context. 768 dimensions.
2019

Sentence-BERT

Full sentence to single vector via siamese networks. 768-1024 dimensions.

Similarity Measures

Three ways to measure how close two vectors are. Drag the arrow tips to see all metrics update in real-time.

Cosine Similarity

1.000
cos(θ) = (A · B) / (|A| |B|)

Angle between vectors. Scale-invariant.

Euclidean Distance

0.000
d = √(Σ(Ai - Bi)²)

Straight-line distance. Sensitive to magnitude.

Dot Product

0.000
A · B = Σ(Ai × Bi)

Combines angle and magnitude. Used in attention.

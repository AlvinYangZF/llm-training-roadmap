Architecture

Chunked Cross-Attention (CCA)

RETRO splits input into 64-token chunks. Standard Transformer layers are interleaved with CCA layers that fuse retrieved neighbor context via cross-attention.

Query from decoder chunk → Cross-Attend to retrieved K/V from neighbors → Fuse external knowledge

RAG — Retrieve-then-Generate

RAG combines a neural retriever (DPR) with a seq2seq generator (BART). Retrieved documents are treated as latent variables.

Innovation

HyDE — Hypothetical Document Embeddings

HyDE generates a hypothetical answer first, then retrieves by doc-doc similarity. The encoder filters hallucinations.

Query → LLM generates hypothetical doc → Encode → Retrieve by doc-doc similarity
